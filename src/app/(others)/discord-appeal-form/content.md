# Information

- It is expected that you write full, detailed responses for the questions requiring it.

- You are expected to respond with full honesty.

- You are not to contact any member of r/alevel Staff regarding the results of your appeal.

- You will not be notified if your appeal is accepted or rejected.

- If you are still unable to join the server after 1 week, assume your appeal was rejected.

- If your ban reason has a date/time period attached to it, please do not submit an appeal until after that date/time period. If you disregard this, your account will be permanently banned from accessing this form.

- If your ban reason states "unappealable", your appeal will be immediately rejected and your account will be banned from accessing this form.

- If you have been caught joining the r/alevel Discord server on an ALT account, your appeal will be immediately rejected and your account will be banned from accessing this form.

- Please ensure you are submitting this form on the account you wish to be unbanned.

- Responses are maxed out at 1024 characters.

- Submitting a troll appeal will lead to your appeal being immediately rejected and your account being banned from accessing this form.
- Submitting identical/heavily similar appeals one after the other will lead to your appeal being immediately rejected and your account being banned from accessing this form.
- Using AI to choreograph or write your responses will lead to your appeal being immediately rejected and your account being banned from accessing this form.
- You may only appeal one case per form. If you are submitting a ban appeal, you must resubmit another appeal to get any warnings removed from your account. Each warning associated to your account (unless it's a double warning for the same offense) should have it's own appeal.
- You are permitted to submit a ban appeal once per week. You may submit timeout/warning appeals whenever you wish.
